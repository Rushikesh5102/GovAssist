## Slide 2: The Problem
**Why do we need GovAssist?**
*   **Information Gap:** Thousands of schemes exist, but citizens don't know which ones apply to them.
*   **Language Barrier:** Official documents are often in English or complex bureaucratic language.
*   **Complex Processes:** Application procedures are confusing and documentation requirements are unclear.
*   **Low Literacy:** Many beneficiaries cannot type or read long textual descriptions.

---

## Slide 3: Our Solution
**GovAssist AI: Your Personal Welfare Companion**
*   **Conversational AI:** Don't search, just ask. Talk to the AI like a human assistant.
*   **Personalized:** Recommendations based on age, income, location, and caste/category.
*   **Accessible:** Native support for local languages (Hindi, Marathi) and Voice interactions.
*   **One-Stop Platform:** Discover, Understand, and Verify documents in one place.

---

## Slide 4: Key Features (1/2)
*   **🤖 Smart Chatbot:** Powered by RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) to provide accurate, fact-checked answers about government schemes.
*   **🗣️ Voice Interaction:** Speak to the app in your local language. Powered by OpenAI Whisper for high-accuracy transcription.
*   **🌐 Multi-Language Support:** Instant toggle between English, Hindi, and Marathi.

---

## Slide 5: Key Features (2/2)
*   **📄 Document Analysis:** Upload photos of documents (Aadhar, Income Cert) for instant validation and data extraction using OCR.
*   **⌨️ Virtual Keyboard:** Built-in regional language keyboard for easy typing.
*   **🛠️ Admin Dashboard:** Management portal for officials to update schemes and manage users.

---

## Slide 6: Technical Architecture
*   **Frontend:** 
    *   React.js with Vite (High Performance).
    *   Tailwind CSS (Modern "Glassmorphism" UI).
    *   Framer Motion (Smooth Animations).
*   **Backend:** 
    *   FastAPI (Python) - High-speed API.
    *   SQLAlchemy & SQLite - Robust Data Management.
*   **AI Engine:** 
    *   Vector Search for relevant scheme retrieval.
    *   Whisper Model for Speech-to-Text.

## Slide 7: User Experience (UI/UX)

# GovAssist AI: User Manual

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **GovAssist AI**! Your personal assistant for navigating Indian Government schemes. This guide will help you get the most out of the platform.

## 2. Getting Started
1.  **Sign Up**: Click "Get Started" and create an account with your email.
2.  **Log In**: Access your dashboard to save your chat history.

## 3. Using the AI Chat
- **Ask Anything**: Type questions like *"What is PM-KISAN?"* or *"Am I eligible for a home loan subsidy?"*.
- **Voice Input**: Click the **Microphone** icon and speak your question. Click again to stop.
- **Language**: Switch between **English**, **Hindi**, and **Marathi** using the buttons at the top right.
- **Virtual Keyboard**: Click the **Keyboard** icon to type in Hindi/Marathi if you don't have a native keyboard.

## 4. Uploading Documents
1.  Click the **Paperclip** icon in the chat bar.
2.  Select a file (PDF, Image, or Word Doc).
3.  The AI will analyze the document and summarize it for you.
4.  You can ask questions about the uploaded file (e.g., *"What is the validity date in this certificate?"*).

## 5. Managing Chats
- **Edit**: Hover over your message and click the **Pencil** icon to correct a mistake.
- **Share**: Click the **Share** icon (top right) to get a link. Send this link to friends or family to show them the conversation.
- **Temporary Chat**: Click the **Clock** icon to start a chat that won't be saved to your history.

## 6. Privacy & Safety
- **Your Data**: We encrypt your personal information.
- **Sensitive Info**: Please do not share passwords or financial PINs in the chat.
- **Accuracy**: AI can make mistakes. Always verify important details with the official links provided.

## 7. Support
If you face any issues, visit the **Contact Us** page or email `support@govassist.in`.
